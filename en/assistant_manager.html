Assistant Manager (AM)

Entry level

GCE A’Level or any equivalent certificates

Objective

By the end of the programme, the trainees should be able to master the method of managerial decisions and skills that bear directly upon the organization’s capacity to survive, to adapt to market and environmental changes, to grow profitably and to move in to new directions as required by the business trends.

Opportunities

The HND holders will be offered positions in all types of companies in their commercial services and human resources.

HND holders in Management can pursue their studies into the Professional Bachelor’s Degree and Professional Master’s Degree at ISFA.
