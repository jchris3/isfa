ACCOUNTANCY

Entry level

GCE A’Level in science and commerce or any equivalent certificates

Objective

The HND programme in Accountancy provides trainees with sound skills and competencies in recording accounts, auditing, taxation and legal aspects, computerized accounting, preparing balance sheet and financial reports.

Opportunities

HND holders in accountancy can work in commercial and industrial companies, banks, accounting firms. They can set-up their own company.

HND holders in accountancy can pursue their studies into the Professional Bachelor’s Degree and Professional Master’s Degree at ISFA.
