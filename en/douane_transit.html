Institut Supérieur des Formations Appliquées

Customs & Transit

Entry Level

GCE A’ Level or any equivalent certificates

Objective

This program trains professionals towards assisting Top executives, businessmen, Logistics & Transports firms in monitoring and managing their logistics supply chain. These trainees play a key-role in coordinating operations within the various departments of the companies and/or those of partners.

Opportunities

HND holders in Customs & Transit can satisfy the demands in any Import/Export companies, accredited customs brokers, banking and financial institutions, diplomatic and international organization. They can be self employed.
HND holders in Customs & Transit can pursue their studies into the Professional Bachelor’s Degree and Professional Master’s Degree at ISFA.
