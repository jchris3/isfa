Welcome address from the Promoter of ISFA

Ladies and gentlemen, dear visitors,
Welcome to the website of the Higher Institute of Applied Trainings (ISFA).

After seventeen years of activities in the vocational training,we have made a decisive step into higher education by creating the Higher Institute of Applied Trainings, as a response to the increasing demand from GCE A’ level holders who seriously want to learn a trade that would enable them earn a living in the socio-professional world. We taught it wise to offer you this learning opportunity at higher education level so that those who could find these valuable trainings only with the Ministry of Employment and Vocational Training, can now get an accurate training in accordance with the Bachelor-Master-Doctorate system.

The Higher Institute of Applied Trainings is a private higher school accredited by the Cameroon ministry in charge of higher education.

ISFA is located at the heart of the economic capital of Cameroon, Douala, especially in the Akwa neighbourhood in a convenient and comfortable environment, appropriate for studies. This strategic geographical position is an asset as there is proximity to the businesses matching our fields of training.

ISFA offers you a range of trainings that lead to the Higher National Diploma (HND) and the Brevet de Technicien Supérieur (BTS).

The trainings taught are in conformity with the new curricula prescribed by the Ministry of Higher Education that best match with the Bachelor-Master-Doctorate system. Moreover, these trainings give access to various passionate careers in the commercial, managerial and legal domains. The diversity of our trainings offer displays our innovative spirit, our sound dynamism and the will to supply the needs of the job market.

The double-plan partnership, national and international, is essential in the training of our students and constitutes an asset in their future careers within companies. This large international opening is enhanced by the presence of teachers and students coming from various foreign countries.

The pedagogic project of ISFA is constructed from the expectations of companies. ISFA works in synergy with the latter in order to ensure trainings that fit their needs. The teaching staff made up of university lecturers and professionals, is dynamic. This makes our training offer evolving and innovative.

This website is designed to be a platform for exchanges that aim at getting you discover our training offer as well as our functioning system, providing answers to your questions and expectations, gathering each and everyone around this noble educational mission.

Ladies and gentlemen, dear visitors, the managing staff of ISFA wish you a nice visit of this website and look forward to welcoming you at our premises soon.

Mesdames, Messieurs, chers internautes, le staff dirigeant de l’Institut Supérieur des Formations Appliquées vous souhaite une bonne visite de ce site et vous fait connaitre, par anticipation le plaisir de vous accueillir au sein de l’Institut.

Best regards
Jean Paul NEGOU
The promoter
